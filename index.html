Talhah Yunus

Rasha kana chi wako

Talhah Yunus is a Pakistani rapper, filmmaker and musician. He graduated from the National College of Arts in filmmaking. He is a part of the hip hop duo Young Stunners along with Talha Anjum, having the music producer Jokhay. He was born and raised in Karachi. He appeared in the PSL Anthem of 2021 "Groove Mera".
